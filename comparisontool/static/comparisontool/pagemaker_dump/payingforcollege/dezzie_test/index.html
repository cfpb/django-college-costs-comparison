Student Debt is on the Rise

For years, student debt has grown at a steady clip, recently surpassing $1trillion. Today, people who leave school with significant debt loads enter a difficult job market. This raises the risk of defaulting on student loans, which can hurt consumers' long-term access to credit. To address student debt issues, it's important to understand the different ways people in diverse circumstances are paying for school.
